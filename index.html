Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):

What is the purpose of the study timer?

The purpose of the study timer is to help you stay focused when you're studying or need to stay focused at work.

How does the study timer work?

The study timer works by setting a countdown timer for a specific study session. During the session, it blocks access to distracting websites and provides gentle reminders to stay focused.

What are the benefits of using the study timer?

The benefits of using the study timer include improved focus, increased productivity, and better time management.

How can I customize the study timer?

You can customize the study timer by setting the duration of each study session, choosing which websites to block, and adjusting the frequency of reminders.

Is the study timer free to use?

Yes, the study timer is free to use. You can download it from our website and start using it right away.

Can I use the study timer on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use the study timer on multiple devices. Simply download and install it on each device you want to use it on.
